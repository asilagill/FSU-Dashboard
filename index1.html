Quick Tips

Author format: Last, F. M. (or Last, F. M., & Other, A. B. for two)

Italics: Journal names, book/report titles are italicized in APA 7

DOIs: Always use https://doi.org/ format

7 or more authors: List first six, then … Last Author

No date: Use (n.d.) for year
